Hook It

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2025

Data Collection

Hook It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no analytics, no advertising, and no accounts.

Local Storage

The app saves your game progress and settings (such as sound preferences and puzzle state) locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs. This data never leaves your device.

Third Parties

Hook It does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Children

Because no data is collected, Hook It is safe for all ages.
