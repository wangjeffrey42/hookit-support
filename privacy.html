Privacy Policy

HookIt  ·  Last updated May 8, 2025

HookIt collects no personal data. Nothing leaves your device.

—

No data collection

The app collects no personal information. There are no accounts, no identifiers, and nothing transmitted off your device.

—

Local storage only

Game progress and settings (puzzle state, move count, sound preference, tutorial status) are saved locally using Unity PlayerPrefs. This data stays on your device.

—

No third parties

No analytics, no ad SDKs, no third-party services of any kind are integrated into the app.

—

Safe for all ages

No data is collected under any circumstances, making HookIt appropriate for users of all ages.

—

Policy changes

Any updates will be posted here with a new date. Significant changes are unlikely given how the app works.

Effective May 8, 2025
