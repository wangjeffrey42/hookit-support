Privacy Policy

Hook It  —  Last updated May 8, 2025

The short version

Hook It collects absolutely no personal data. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics, no ads. Everything stays on your device.

🚫

No Data Collection

Hook It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no user accounts, no login screens, and no identifiers of any kind sent off your device.

📱

Local Storage Only

The app saves your game progress and preferences — including your current puzzle state, move count, sound setting, and tutorial status — locally on your device using Unity PlayerPrefs. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone else.

🤝

No Third Parties

Hook It does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics platforms. There are no trackers of any kind embedded in the app.

📵

No Ads

Hook It contains no advertisements and no ad SDKs. You will never see a banner, interstitial, or rewarded ad.

🧒

Safe for All Ages

Because no data is collected under any circumstances, Hook It is appropriate for users of all ages, including children.

📝

Policy Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Given the nature of the app, significant changes are unlikely.
